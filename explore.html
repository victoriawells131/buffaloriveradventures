Things to Do

Here at Buffalo River Adventures, we encourage you to make the most out of your trip no matter how big or small of a group. A trip to Buffalo National River provides opportunities to add a diverse array of activities in addition to your stay. Whether you take up a guided tour, horseback riding, or mountain biking, there is something here for everyone. Come share our love of the outdoors with your friends and family!

Guided Tours

We offer a number of activities where we can show you around the park. Whether it's a guided hike or an oppourtunity for your young ones to be a junior ranger! Our expert rangers are eager to share their knowledge about the area.

Canoeing

As spring blossoms, so does paddling. We have levels for everyone, those who like a challenge can seek interest in our disricts and decide which to start their journey at. It's important to be aware of the river levels, as we priotize the safety of all of customers.
